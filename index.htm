List of Registry Game Templates

I have put together a number of templates to help foster ideas for the look and feel of our Registry Game. The idea behind the templates is to show different layout options and spark ideas. The templates can be combined, modified, or completely disregarded as we move forward with the UI.

Please note that all templates feature "Advance Me" and "Demote Me" links that exist to help give you a feel for how the template's color will change with level advancement.

Enjoy and let me know what you think!

  • Generic Template - Quickly developed to push pages out and decide on UI later
  • Dojo Template - Based on an conversation during a walk - the idea is cartoon martial artist who seeks self improvement
  • Building Template - The game exists to help build better people by helping them understand the impacts of choices
